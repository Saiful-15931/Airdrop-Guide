BitQuant
// ai-powered defi agent
⚡ LIVE NOW
BitQuant is OpenGradient's flagship AI agent for DeFi analytics, trading, and portfolio management. It uses natural language — simply ask questions in plain English and get instant AI-powered insights on crypto markets and DeFi protocols.
// Step-by-step guide
01Go to bitquant.io ↗
02Connect your wallet — use MetaMask or Rabby Wallet
03Ask AI questions like: "What is the current risk profile on Bitcoin?" or "Show me top DeFi yields"
04Use it every day to accumulate points — more interaction = more points
05Refer friends to earn bonus points on top of daily activity
✅ Reward: Points from daily interaction + referrals

Twin.fun
// digital ai twins platform
🔥 POINTS FARMING
Twin.fun is a social platform where real creators and investors have AI digital twins. Buy Twin Keys to chat with their AI and earn points on the leaderboard. Every buy or sell trade earns you points. Live on BNB Chain.
// Step-by-step guide
01Go to twin.fun ↗
02Connect your wallet → Switch to BNB Chain (Mainnet)
03Buy a Twin Key — start with a small amount to test
04Chat with the Twin — ask questions and explore their AI
05Every Buy/Sell trade earns points. Invite friends = 100 bonus points per invite!
✅ Reward: Points per trade + 100pts per invite
